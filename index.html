Juicy instead of bloody?

Food and climate change are linked, so eat low carboN :

  • Move away from beef and lamb.
  • Don't waste food.
  • Concentrate on regional and seasonal food.
  • Don't eat preprocessed and packed food.
  • Don't use products with palm oil.

I like my bike !

There is always a reason to go by bike instead of a car:

  • It isn't that cold.
  • It isn't raining that much.
  • It isn't that windy
  • Any of the above?

The power is Yours !

It is somehow positive to stay on a little bit darker side:

  • Turn off unnecessary lights.
  • Unplug unused electronics.
  • Be efficient with refrigeration-think before you reach.
  • Hang dry your laundry instead of using dryier
